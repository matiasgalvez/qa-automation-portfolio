QA approach

How I approach quality engineering

01

Understand business risk

I identify critical flows such as login, checkout, payments, backend integrations and release blockers.

02

Define test coverage

I separate smoke, regression, exploratory, API, backend and mobile coverage based on release risk.

03

Automate high-value checks

I prioritize stable, repeatable and business-critical scenarios for automation.

04

Integrate with CI/CD

I use quality gates such as PR smoke tests, daily regression runs and visible test reports.

05

Improve continuously

I review failures, reduce flakiness, improve testability and mentor QA engineers on maintainable practices.
